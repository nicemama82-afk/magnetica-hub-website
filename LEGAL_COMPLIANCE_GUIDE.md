Creators should agree to:
- Original content ownership
- No copyright infringement
- Compliance with laws
- Accurate product descriptions
- Quality standards
- Payment terms
- Dispute resolution

**Template:**
```
CREATOR AGREEMENT

This agreement is between [Creator Name] and Magnetica Hub.

1. Content Ownership
Creator warrants that all content uploaded is original or properly licensed.

2. Compliance
Creator agrees to comply with all applicable laws and Magnetica Hub policies.

3. Accuracy
Creator agrees that all product descriptions and claims are accurate and not misleading.

4. Quality
Creator agrees to maintain quality standards for all products and services.

5. Payments
Payments will be processed according to Magnetica Hub's payment schedule.

6. Termination
Magnetica Hub may terminate this agreement for violation of these terms.

By uploading content, Creator agrees to these terms.
```

## Compliance by Jurisdiction

### United States
**Key Laws:**
- FTC Act (consumer protection)
- CAN-SPAM Act (email marketing)
- CCPA (California privacy)
- COPPA (children's privacy)

**Actions:**
- Include unsubscribe link in emails
- Comply with CCPA rights
- Don't collect data from children under 13
- Be truthful in advertising

### European Union
**Key Laws:**
- GDPR (data protection)
- ePrivacy Directive
- Consumer Rights Directive

**Actions:**
- Get explicit consent for data collection
- Provide data access/deletion rights
- Appoint Data Protection Officer
- Conduct privacy impact assessments
- Report breaches within 72 hours

### Canada
**Key Laws:**
- PIPEDA (privacy)
- CASL (email marketing)

**Actions:**
- Get express or implied consent
- Include clear unsubscribe option
- Identify sender clearly
- Respond to privacy requests

### Australia
**Key Laws:**
- Privacy Act
- Australian Consumer Law

**Actions:**
- Comply with privacy principles
- Provide privacy policy
- Handle complaints
- Protect consumer rights

## Email Marketing Compliance

### CAN-SPAM Act (USA)
Your marketing emails must include:
- [ ] Clear identification of sender
- [ ] Accurate subject line
- [ ] Physical address
- [ ] Unsubscribe link
- [ ] Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 days

### GDPR (Europe)
- [ ] Get explicit opt-in consent
- [ ] Maintain consent records
- [ ] Provide easy opt-out
- [ ] Honor opt-out immediately

### CASL (Canada)
- [ ] Get express or implied consent
- [ ] Identify sender clearly
- [ ] Include physical address
- [ ] Provide unsubscribe mechanism
- [ ] Honor unsubscribe within 10 days

## Content Moderation Policy

### Prohibited Content
- Illegal content
- Copyrighted material without permission
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Harassment or abuse
- Sexually explicit content
- Misinformation about health/safety
- Spam or phishing
- Malware or viruses

### Moderation Process
1. Report submission
2. Review by moderation team
3. Determination of violation
4. Content removal if necessary
5. Creator notification
6. Appeal process

## Dispute Resolution

### Process
1. **Informal Resolution** (30 days)
   - Direct communication
   - Good faith negotiation
   - Mediation if needed

2. **Formal Resolution** (if informal fails)
   - Arbitration or court proceedings
   - Governed by specified jurisdiction
   - Binding or non-binding as specified

### Include in Terms
- Jurisdiction for disputes
- Arbitration vs. litigation
- Costs and attorney fees
- Appeal process

## Insurance Recommendations

### Consider Getting
1. **General Liability Insurance**
   - Covers bodily injury and property damage
   - $1-2 million coverage recommended

2. **Professional Liability Insurance**
   - Covers errors and omissions
   - Important for health/wellness content

3. **Cyber Liability Insurance**
   - Covers data breaches
   - Covers privacy violations
   - $1-5 million coverage recommended

4. **Employment Practices Liability Insurance**
   - If you have employees
   - Covers employment-related claims
